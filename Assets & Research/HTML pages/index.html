Clean Water and Sanitation


Clean Water for a Sustainable Future

Access to safe water and sanitation is a fundamental human right. Yet millions of people around the world still face water scarcity, contamination, and inadequate hygiene services. Our mission is to highlight the importance of Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6) and inspire action toward a world where clean water and sanitation are available for all.



Why SDG 6 Matters

  • 785 million people still lack basic drinking water services.
  • Poor sanitation contributes to the spread of disease and impacts education, economic opportunity, and community well-being.
  • Sustainable water management protects ecosystems and strengthens climate resilience.


What We Do

We promote awareness, share innovations, and showcase global efforts that help ensure access to safe and affordable drinking water, improve sanitation, and protect freshwater ecosystems. Through education and collaboration, we move closer to a cleaner, healthier world.


How You Can Make an Impact

Creating sustainable access to clean water and sanitation requires the involvement of people everywhere. Small, everyday actions—like conserving water at home, supporting organizations that protect rivers and wetlands, or learning about global water challenges—can contribute to real, long-term change. Communities, schools, and businesses also play a vital role by adopting water-efficient technologies, promoting hygiene education, and reducing pollution. By staying informed and participating in local or global initiatives, anyone can help build a future where safe water and sanitation are guaranteed for all.
